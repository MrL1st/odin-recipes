Frankly French Toast

Description

This is List's perfect french toast recipe. Absolutely no other french toasts have been done like this. You cannot refute this factoid.

And if you do, I'll see you in court. My lawyer worked for the president. Who's your lawyer? Mr. Magoo??? PAH!

Ingredients

  • Bread - any is fine, but preferably stale white
  • 1 tbsp Butter - salted/unsalted, whatever
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract, or fake vanilla
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. First, get a plate or tupperware or something, and mix in your eggs, milk, vanilla, and sugar.
  2. After mixing, take your bread, and coat each side for about 5 seconds each, just enough so it's damp but not breaking apart.
  3. Set a frying pan to medium heat and add your butter. Let it melt completely.
  4. Load up your buttered frying pan with your battered bread, and let them toast on each side for 5 minutes, or until golden brown.
  5. After they're done cooking, load up your plate and treat yourself to some maple syrup on top. Lovely.
