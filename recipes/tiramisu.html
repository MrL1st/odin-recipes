Tiramisu in Cups

Quick, easy, ovenless tiramisu. This classic Italian coffee dessert is a bonafide crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 250g mascarpone
  • 4 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 3 tbsp brandy or cognac
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 150ml whipping cream
  • 200ml cold espresso
  • 4 sponge finger cookies
  • Ground coffee and grated frozen dark chocolate

Steps

  1. Mix together in a bowl the powdered sugar and mascarpone, and beat together with brandy and vanilla extract.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the cream until you reach the soft peak stage.
  3. Mix the cream and marscapone mix together.
  4. Break the sponge fingers in two and dip them into the espresso very briefly.
  5. Place sponge fingers into a small serving bowl and cover with the mascarpone mixture, then refrigerate for 2-24 hours.
  6. Add ground coffee and grated chocolate on top for garnish before serving.
