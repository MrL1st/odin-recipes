Fries with Ketchup and Mayo

This classic dish can be eaten as a side or a full meal. Best eaten hot with a sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs peeled russet potatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp onion salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 0.5 cup water (or as needed)

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes into fries and place them into a large bowl of cold water to prevent browning.
  2. Heat oil in a saucepan or skillet to 175 C/350 F.
  3. While heating the oil, mix together the flour, salt, garlic salt, onion salt, and paprika. Gradually stir in water to make the mixture thick enough to be drizzled from a spoon.
  4. Drain the water from the fries and pat dry. Then, dip each fry into the batter one at a time.
  5. Carefully lower the fries into the hot oil in batches. Fry them until they're golden brown on each side, about 8-10 minutes.
  6. Drain oil from the fries on a paper towel or on a metal rack. Serve hot and enjoy.
