Split Pea Soup in a Bowl

Dutch-style split pea soup. The perfect remedy for any rainy day.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 celery ribs, diced
  • 0.5 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 pound dried split peas, rinsed
  • 1 pound cooked ham or hot dog, diced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 qt chicken stock
  • 2.5 cups of water
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
